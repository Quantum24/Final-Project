$35/day

I'm willing to watch your dog at my house or mine. Either way, I charge $35 per day, with special rates available for extended periods and multiple dogs.

Food not provided

If you aren't able to provide food for your dog, the cost of food and anything else is added onto the cost.

Tips accepted

Tips are not expected but are appreciated.

Provide necessities

If your dog has a crate, bed, food or water bowl, leash, or favorite toys, you are welcome to bring them.
